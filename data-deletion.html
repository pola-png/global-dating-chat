Data Deletion Policy for Global Dating Chat

Account Deletion Process

Users can request complete deletion of their account and associated data through the following methods:

Method 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the Global Dating Chat app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Account
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion request

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to globaldatingchat@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

What Gets Deleted

Upon account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your profile information (name, photos, bio)
  • All messages sent and received
  • Posts and interactions (likes, comments)
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Authentication data

Third-Party Data

Note: Data collected by advertising partners (Google AdMob) is governed by their respective privacy policies. To delete advertising data:

  • Visit Google Ad Settings to manage your advertising profile
  • Reset your Advertising ID in device settings
  • Contact advertising partners directly for complete data removal

Data Deletion Timeline

  • Immediate: Account becomes inaccessible
  • Within 7 days: Profile removed from all user searches
  • Within 30 days: Complete data purging from all systems
  • Backup systems: Cleared within 90 days

Data That May Be Retained

For legal and safety reasons, we may retain:

  • Reports of violations or abuse (anonymized)
  • Transaction records (if applicable)
  • Data required by law enforcement or legal proceedings
  • Aggregated analytics data (non-personally identifiable)

Advertising Data: Data collected by Google AdMob and other advertising partners is subject to their retention policies and must be deleted through their respective systems.

Confirmation

You will receive email confirmation when:

  1. Deletion request is received
  2. Account deactivation is complete
  3. Final data purging is finished

Contact for Deletion Requests

Email: globaldatingchat@gmail.com
Response Time: Within 48 hours

This policy ensures compliance with GDPR Article 17 (Right to Erasure) and Google Play Store data deletion requirements.
