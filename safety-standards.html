Child Safety & Protection Standards

Our Commitment

At Global Dating Chat, we are fully committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users. We strictly prohibit any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and cooperate with international and regional law enforcement agencies to report and remove such material immediately.

Our Safety Standards

  • Age Restriction: Global Dating Chat is strictly for users aged 18 years and older. We use verification tools to detect and prevent underage access.
  • Content Monitoring: All reported and flagged content is promptly reviewed by trained moderators. Any content or behavior involving or suggesting CSAE is immediately escalated for removal and reporting.
  • Reporting Tools: Users can easily report inappropriate content, profiles, or messages directly within the app.
  • Moderator Training: Our moderation team receives ongoing training to identify, report, and respond to any form of CSAM or CSAE activity.
  • Law Enforcement Cooperation: We work closely with national and regional authorities to report any verified instances of child exploitation or abuse.
  • Data Protection: We maintain strict confidentiality and comply with international data protection laws to ensure user safety and privacy.

Reporting Suspected Abuse

If you encounter or suspect any content involving child sexual exploitation or abuse, please report it immediately through our in-app reporting tools or contact us directly.
