Privacy Policy for Global Dating Chat

Information We Collect

Usage Data

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure

  • Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest

Your Rights

  • Export your data in a portable format

Ad Personalization Control

You can control ad personalization by:

  • Visiting Google Ad Settings
  • Using your device's "Limit Ad Tracking" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" setting
